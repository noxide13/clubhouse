List of Features:

Members:

View clubs listing
List out and browse through all clubs that have registered on Clubhouse

Receive push notifications from clubs
Push notifications for updates and/or reminders for events

Register for membership
Customizable registration linked that can be added to a club's profile page

Synchronise calendar from Clubhouse to 3rd party program
Sync event dates and such with your calendar (eg. Google Calendar, iCal)

One account for club application
One account allows you to register for multiple clubs with auto-fill options for personal details

View news archive
Viewing of past news and/or events

Club Administrators:

List their clubs
Ability to register their clubs on Clubhouse so that they would appear on the club listing for user viewing

Post news and updates
Clubs may update their list of events and meetings so as to notify any registered members using Clubhouse

Customize registration link
Clubs may list out any external link to their registration method (eg. Google Forms)

View membership list and details
Allows the browsing of all members that have registered for a particular club via Clubhouse

View news archive
Viewing of past news and/or events
