A little about us.

Clubhouse

Clubhouse. What is it? Clubhouse is a one-stop platform for anyone to go to for any news regarding clubs and societies. Searching for a new club but you have no idea what you have in mind? Not a problem. Already know/heard of a club you're interested in but have no idea where to start the registration process? Not a problem. Already a member of a club? Even better! There are many functionalities for existing club members too. We have it all:

  • Browse/search Clubs & Societies
  • Online registration
  • Viewing of club events (both past and future)
  • Push notifications for club events
  • Calendar synchronization for club events
  • Viewing of club member lists
  • Viewing of club merchandise

What are you waiting for? Download the app now and be ready to have your mind blown.








Mission

To empower and unite all clubs and societies and bring communities together.

Vision

Our vision is that we will grow to eventually include and connect all clubs and socities - be they private or public - globally.
